Adegbite Olaoluwa

I’m a freelance multi-disciplinary graphic designer who’s delivered creative and engaging solutions across brand identity, print, packaging, and digital media. In 2013, my online brand campaign for the Dorsey Children’s Hospital won a GDUSA award, one of the most prestigious honors in the graphic design industry. My work has also been featured in Creatique Bloq, Compound Magazine, and on the Creative Review blog.
